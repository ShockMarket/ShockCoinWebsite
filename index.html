What is ShockCoin

ShockCoin is a form of digital currency, created and held electronically. No one controls it. ShockCoins aren’t printed, like dollars or euros – they’re produced by people running computers all around the world, using software that solves mathematical problems.

How to earn ShockCoin

Where do ShockCoins come from? With paper money, a government decides when to print and distribute money. ShockCoin doesn't have a central government, instead, it is issued by the network.

With ShockCoin, miners use special software to solve math problems and are issued a certain number of ShockCoins in exchange. This provides a smart way to issue the currency and also creates an incentive for more people to mine.

Price

The price of ShockCoin is based on supply and demand. This is ultimately what determines the price.
